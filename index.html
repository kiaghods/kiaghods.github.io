Kia Ghods

cs + math @ princeton

kia.ghods [at] princeton (dot) edu

I am a second-year undergraduate at Princeton University , studying cs + math. I am broadly interested in the intersection of machine learning, mathematics, and cognitive science.

I am a researcher in Princeton’s Princeton Language and Intelligence Initiative , where I work on mechanistic interpretability and alignment of large language models and vision-language models. I am advised by Professors Jon Cohen and Tom Griffiths .

At Princeton, I am President of Princeton’s ACM chapter, Officer of AI@Princeton, and a member/co-organizer of various machine learning related reading groups. I previously interned at Hillspire and have worked at early-stage startups (i.e., Astralis).

Outside of academics, I am an avid fan of both playing and watching basketball and tennis. I also enjoy reading and writing.
